getNexusArtifactList(nexusUrl, repositoryName, artifactGroup, artifactName)
    nexusUrl is url for nexus, for example - https://nexus-nexus.d1czos.ifortuna.cz
    repositoryName is repository, for example - ifortunasnapshot, ifortunarelease
    artifactGroup is artifact group, for example - com.etnetera
    artifactName is artifact name, for example - jnp_4_fortuna, kralikarna
